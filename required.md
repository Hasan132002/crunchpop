Create a complete wireframe for a 3-page CrunchPop Candy website section inside the larger Field &amp; Pantry brand.

This should be a fun, colorful freeze-dried candy website, but it must still connect clearly to Field &amp; Pantry LLC’s larger mission: South Florida hurricane preparedness, shelf-stable food, practical emergency pantries, and helping families prepare before they need it.

The site should not feel scary, survivalist, military, or overly serious. It should feel bright, local, friendly, fun, trustworthy, and easy to shop.

Website structure:

1. Home / Mission Page
2. Candy Shop Page
3. Custom Orders / Events Page

Overall design direction:

* Bright candy colors
* Clean layout
* Big product photos
* Easy buttons
* Mobile-first design
* Fun but not childish
* Mission-driven but not heavy
* Simple navigation
* Fast shopping flow
* Clear connection to Field &amp; Pantry
* Use rounded cards, colorful accents, product image sections, and clean readable text
* Avoid clutter
* Avoid dark survival/prepper design
* Avoid making the mission feel depressing or fear-based

Main navigation:

Important brand message:

Secondary Goal: Build the Early Field &amp; Pantry Customer List

A secondary goal of the CrunchPop Candy website is to collect early interest for the larger Field &amp; Pantry mission.

CrunchPop Candy is the product available now, but Field &amp; Pantry is building toward future shelf-stable food, hurricane pantry products, and custom freeze-drying services. The website should quietly collect names, emails, and interest areas from visitors who want to follow that larger mission.

This should be done in a natural, unobtrusive way through signup sections, interest forms, custom order forms, and “be first to know” calls to action. The goal is not to block people from shopping, but to start building an early audience before Field &amp; Pantry begins producing its preparedness food line.

The signup should help identify what people are interested in, such as:

* freeze-dried candy
* hurricane pantry products
* custom freeze-drying
* family emergency food
* local South Florida updates
* fundraising or group orders
* wholesale interest
